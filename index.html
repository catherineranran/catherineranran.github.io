About Me

PhD candidate @Free University Amsterdam. With the belief that 'Serious inquiry into human nature is not a luxury', I am inspecting how dispositional factors such as personality traits and contextual factors jointly shape human behavior, notably, prosocial and (im)moral behavior.

Incoming PostDoc @Max Planck Institute for the Study of Crime, Security and Law.

(Personality-Social) Psychology is 'Forever Moonlight'. I additionally have interdisciplinary interests, e.g., computational social science, behavioral economics, philosophy, and criminology.
